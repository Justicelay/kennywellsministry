About Kenny Wells Ministry

Our Vision

To be a beacon of God's light, transforming lives through the Gospel of Jesus Christ and establishing a global community of believers united in faith, purpose, and service.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to:

  • Sharing the Gospel of the Kingdom of Jesus Christ with clarity and compassion
  • Nurturing spiritual growth through Biblical teaching
  • Serving communities with Christ's love
  • Empowering believers for effective ministry

For over two decades, Kenny Wells Ministry has been dedicated to sharing the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Founded by Pastor Kenny Wells in 2000.

Our ministry began with a simple mission: to help people discover God's love and purpose for their lives. Through powerful Biblical teaching, community outreach, and humanitarian efforts, we've touched countless lives and witnessed remarkable transformations.
